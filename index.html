Everything.io

An announcement

What we're about to announce will probably not bode well financially for the company, but it needs to be done.

Everything.io is a company that makes money on things. We succeed because we appeal to your need to consume and have new products and services in your life. But we all know there is more to life than money and things.

I started Everything for the same reasons anyone would start a business, but as its owner, I'm not a walking corporation. I'm a human who has endured hardship in my life. I've made decisions I can never take back. I've lost people who meant so much to me without really appreciating the time I had with them. I can't look back at the end of my life and see only a company that sold some stuff as my contribution to the world.

So starting now, we will suspend all our operations all month to make a statement about compassion.

I'm asking whomever this message can reach to just take 5 minutes to stop buying, stop stressing, stop being so busy and tell those close to you that you love them.

Think of the phone call you wish you had made long ago. Have dinner with your family. Say whatever you need to say...ask for forgiveness, give appreciation, remind them how much they mean to you.

I'm hoping you can also help by sending this message to your networks so others will do the same.
